# Reflection

This assessment was designed to reinforce engineering thinking rather than command memorization.

A perfect score demonstrates understanding of:

- Evidence collection
- Structured investigations
- Resource analysis
- Business-aware troubleshooting
- Verification of recovery

The goal is not simply to know Linux commands, but to develop the judgment required to solve production problems with confidence.
